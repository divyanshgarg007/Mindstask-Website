Why does your business need a mobile app?

Mobile app market is thriving fast. The number of smartphone users across the globe are going to hit 3.5 Billion. Quick facts that tell why more and more businesses are investing in mobile apps:

  1. Mobile apps are expected to generate over $935 billion in revenue by 2023. (Statista)
  2. Nearly 90% of mobile internet time is spent in apps. (eMarketer)
  3. Most users have more than 10 apps installed on their phones. (Statista)
  4. Smartphones now account for 70% of US digital media time.

In the global Covid pandemic, most businesses are going digital, and enterprise mobility plays a key role.

What services mobile app development company offers?

A mobile app development company generally offers end-to-end mobile app development services that include mobile app strategy, mobile UI/UX design, app development, app QA &amp; testing, app integration and deployment, and app maintenance and support for iOS, Android, and the web. A mobile app development company also offers cross platform app development services such as Flutter app development and React Native app development. Some also offer services around optimizing mobile apps for higher visibility on app stores and increasing genuine downloads of the app.

Why choose a dedicated mobile application development services provider?

Mobile app development team includes mobile app analysts, UI/UX experts, creative designers, mobile app testers, DevOps engineers, and cybersecurity experts. Every person has a special role in making your mobile app successful. A dedicated application development services provider has vast expertise with mobile app development tools and technologies, SDK kits, App store rules and guidelines, and other mobile development platforms like Flutter and React Native. You know different mobile app projects will require different skills, so they have expert teams in all disciplines required for mobile app development of any type and size.

Should you hire specialists to deploy your mobile app? 

Yes, you should hire a specialist to deploy your app. Or your mobile apps team should have at least one person who understands how to deploy apps. Organizations can also hire "DevOps" who think like "developers" and "developers" who think like "operations." Simply, you need specialists that not only understand issues on both sides of the historical IT divide, but in all probability could perform well in either function.
